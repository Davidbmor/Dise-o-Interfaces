Biography

Cristian "Kaze" is a 24-year-old MC from Cartagena who started rapping at the age of 13. At 16, he performed alongside international artists like Gordo Master and Sho-Hai. By 18, he gained recognition on digital platforms with hits like “No saben a quien tienen.”.

In 2015, he participated in the successful "Circuito de Utopía" tour alongside Hard GZ and Nikone. In 2016, he released his first physical album, "No Encajes," which sold out on launch day, and began his first solo tour, filling filling important venues across Spain. Kaze's powerful lyrics and energetic performances resonate with audiences, making him a rising star in the scene.

Kaze continues to evolve as an artist, blending various musical styles and pushing the boundaries of his craft. His dedication to the genre and connection with fans have solidified his reputation in the Spanish rap scene..

LAST ALBUM

Since mid-year, the artist had been hinting that by the end of the year we might finally have new music in our hands. And so it has been. The marketing progress for this project has been moving both slowly and swiftly at the same time. The meticulous attention to detail and the journey of evolution can be seen in each teaser we've been allowed to glimpse. The beginning of this new era arrived in early September, when, in the midst of his last shows before the album release, he decided to radically change his social media presence and introduce his new logo. He left behind the iconic name “Kaze” underlined by the letter K, replacing it with a new design where K and Z are joined.
